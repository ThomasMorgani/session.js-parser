Phased Reopening

We continue to plan towards expanding the library services available to you. Our top priority, as always, is the health and safety of our patrons and staff. We will continue following the rulings and recommendations of our local, state and federal leadership throughout this process, working alongside health and safety officials and our colleagues to safely serve you. Based upon comments from the Governor and other officials, it seems that Long Island is close to reaching Phase 1 of the statewide reopening plan. However, while we we remain closed, please do not return library materials in our book drop, fines will not be assessed for overdue materials while the Library is closed. Please check this website for continued updates on the library’s status.
